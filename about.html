Our Story

The Beginning

Founded in 2020, Akari Store emerged from a passion for Filipino fashion and culture. Our name "Akari" means "light" in Filipino, symbolizing our mission to illuminate the world with unique fashion pieces that blend traditional Filipino aesthetics with modern design.

Our Journey

What started as a small boutique in Manila has grown into an international fashion brand, bringing the essence of Filipino style to fashion enthusiasts worldwide. We believe in creating pieces that not only look beautiful but also tell a story and connect people across cultures.

Our Commitment

Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and innovation drives everything we do. We work closely with local artisans and designers to create collections that honor Filipino craftsmanship while embracing contemporary fashion trends.
